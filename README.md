# Superstore Sales Dashboard Analysis

## 📌 Short Description

An interactive Power BI dashboard created to analyze sales, profit, products, and customer behavior from 2011–2014. The dashboard helps identify business growth trends, top-performing products, and regional sales insights.

---

# 🛠 Tech Stack

* 📊 <span>Microsoft Power BI</span>
* 📂 Power Query
* 🧠 DAX
* 📝 Data Modeling

---

# 📁 Data Source

Dataset sourced from <span>Kaggle</span> — Superstore Sales Dataset containing sales, profit, orders, customers, products, and regional data from 2011–2014.

---

# ✨ Dashboard Highlights

## 📌 Executive Dashboard

* Total Sales, Profit, Orders & Quantity KPIs
* Sales by Market & Country
* Monthly Sales Trend

### Insights

* 📈 Sales grew yearly
* 🛒 Q4 had highest sales
* 🌍 APAC & EU led sales

---

## 📌 Product Analysis

* Product Sales & Profit KPIs
* Sales vs Profit Analysis
* Product Performance Table

### Insights

* 📦 Office Supplies sold most units
* 📈 Profit improved after 2012
* 🏆 Phones generated high sales

---

## 📌 Customer Behavior

* Customer & Repeat Customer KPIs
* Orders by Country & Category
* Monthly Order Trend

### Insights

* 👥 Repeat customers increased
* 🌍 US had highest orders
* 🛍 December showed peak buying

---

# 📊 Business Impact

* Identified yearly sales growth
* Analyzed customer purchasing trends
* Highlighted top-performing products
* Supported data-driven business decisions

* Understand customer behavior
* Improve strategic business planning
